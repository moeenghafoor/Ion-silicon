Industrial standard basis

Our services include analogue circuit design and simulation, as well as digital circuit design and simulation, ensuring efficient electronic systems. We specialize in high-speed PCB design, accommodating standards such as DDR3, PCIe, HDMI, LVDS, Ethernet, and USB3 to meet the demands of modern, high-performance applications. Our capabilities extend to mixed-signal PCB design, seamlessly integrating analogue and digital components to create versatile and efficient circuits. We ensure that all our PCB designs are EMC benchmarked. Our high-density interconnect (HDI) PCB design services provide compact and complex board layouts, maximizing functionality in minimal space.

Circuit and PCB simulation

High-speed digital designs, RF systems, and complex power systems require an extra step beyond simulation, analysis, and testing. We perform these simulations using the appropriate tools to ensure that your design meets your expectations, the project obtains the necessary certifications, and the product endures throughout its market lifetime.
